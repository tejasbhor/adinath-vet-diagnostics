Laboratory Commissioning

End-to-End Diagnostic Lab Setup & Integration

Setting up an animal disease testing lab requires precise workflow segregation to prevent template carryover, cross-contamination, and false positives. We provide turnkey services designing and configuring clinical labs from the ground up, complying with veterinary biosafety requirements.

We manage biological workflow layouts, select compatible instrumentation portfolios, install and calibrate biosafety cabinets, and provide training for lab technicians on assay execution.

Hands-on assay run guidance, laboratory safety protocols, maintenance cycles training, and continuous supply chains setup for reagents.
